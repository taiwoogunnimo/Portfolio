About Me

My name is Taiwo Timilehin Ogunnimo, I currently anchor a radio show at Phoenix Radio (CUSU, Coventry) titled “Spill the Tea with Tee”. I generally talk about the media reality and the actual reality in fashion, relationship, culture, lifestyle, and education. I am a short filmmaker with two successfully produced short films on YouTube - (TwoTees Productions channel). As a creative writer, I have written numerous pieces both offline and online (Medium and Vocal Media), and I occasionally write autobiographies for elites in Nigeria.

I am a graduate of English studies from the prestigious Babcock University, Nigeria. I advanced my education with a master’s in English Literature at the University of Lagos, Nigeria. And with my growing love and passion for media, I decided to get an advanced master's degree in Communication, Culture, and Media at Coventry University, United Kingdom.

I have freelanced as a creative director for different organizations where I led the team who developed concepts for branding and promotions, pitched ideas to customers, and executed ideas for the growth of the organization and the satisfaction of our clients. I worked as a brand and communication executive at TrustBanc Group, where I oversaw and implemented all promotional and brand marketing activities to establish and maintain the brand's presence in various locations. I coordinated designs for the organization's branding materials for both online and offline channels leading to the successful onboarding of prospective customers, and coordinated with product heads of various departments to understand their needs and design effective branding strategies.

I have also worked as a creative director and a content strategist at SceneOne Productions, where I create regular video contents and social media posts designed to target larger audiences and viewers. Conducting interviews and working with prolific professionals within the various fashion, music, and film departments in the Nigerian entertainment landscape. Working collaboratively with groups across the network, including the digital/social, affiliate, and programming teams. Conceiving, writing, producing, and editing promotions for films, series, sports events, and special presentations. Additionally, formulations of scripts for short movies, running Tv series, social media content, and Advertisements.
